What is Nature to Us?

The relationship between humans and nature is one of deep connection and mutual dependence. We rely on nature for the things that keep us alive, like air, water, food, and shelter. At the same time, everything we do affects the natural world around us. This connection is not just about survival but also about our responsibility to take care of the environment that supports us.

Nature gives us the resources and balance we need to live, while our actions can either protect or harm it. When people live in harmony with nature, both sides benefit and thrive. But when we ignore or abuse it, problems like pollution, climate change, and loss of wildlife begin to grow.

In simple terms, humans and nature are partners. We are part of the environment, not separate from it, and understanding this helps us build a better, healthier world for everyone.
